Product Page design

Google Workspace

Unlocking the wow factor of the e-mail product and premiere partnership.

Showcasing premiere products

Creating a landing page to advertise the google workspace product and partnership was the creative ask. We were also given the insight that our sister brands would adopt the same design format once this project was finished.

There were key features of the Google Workspace that marketing wanted to showcase. Calendar integration, access to cloud apps, and accessing your information anywhere were the main selling points. After the UX was approved I worked with my Art director and team members to design the final look of the product page.

The final design of the product page was adopted by the other umbrella brands. i.e. Register.com, Web.com, and others.
