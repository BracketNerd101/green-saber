Hey There!

I’m Arthur Charles Edwards but my hockey teammates call me Ace. It's a nickname that my family gave me early on that initially stuck. I am originally from Gainesville, Florida but have spent the last few years in Jacksonville. I am a Visual Designer and Front-End Enthusiast who graduated from SCAD-Atlanta with a B.F.A. Since graduating, I’ve designed large-scale web applications, honed Front-End development, and created work that affects millions of people across the world. When I am not thinking about design I enjoy chess, hockey, and scenic photography.

A recent professional goal of mine is to travel to various international design conventions to learn more about the global influence of design. I’m available to learn more about design positions, tennis dates and coffee.

p.s. I'm proud to share that I built my website with SASS/SCSS and Bootstrap 5.
