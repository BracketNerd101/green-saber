Digital Re Branding

Network Solutions

Creating a visual playground for a legendary brand in the internet industry.

Modernizing a pioneer of the interent

Known as the first domain registrar, Network Solutions has dramatically impacted the internet industry. How does the brand continue to stay relevant in the modern design meta?

Designing this brand took a lot of research about the company's target audience. These tech-savvy professionals know what they want. This gave us the green light to focus on the brand concept instead of the products themselves. The concept of the golden ratio grid was a critical part of the redesign process. The combination of color grid placement allows the brand to have a timeless foundation with modern appeal.

I worked with my Creative Directors Lexy DeRosa and Matt Hannon. They provided a lot of positive energy and focused feedback when developing these concepts.
