Florida Blue

Designing a seamless payment process that empowers the customer.

Billing Application

Florida Blue is an insurance company serving 15 million people across the U.S.A. The billing application for customers was in need of a major redesign. The redesign included the need to add a new user flow as well as modern visual treatments.

To help the user feel confident in the end-to-end payment process, a new design system was created. These additions allow the customer to pay with a variety of tailored options that best meet their needs. I worked with my manager, Eric Crawford; Ashli Thomas, Pankaj Bhagwat, and a team of developers to create the application.
